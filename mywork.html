RTS 3D

This is the first cross-platform I've created, able to run on a Windows PC, but also on any Raspberry Pi; a cheaper way of becoming familiar with console-programming. I gained experience in hardware rendering and OpenGL. More advanced topics, such as instancing, animations and procedural textures were used. I learned my way around ImGui and the Bullet Physics library. The terrain and forests are procedurally generated, with a basic level editor to tweak the parameters as needed. The entire gamestate can be serialized to a compressed file, to be loaded in again at a later time.

2023

2D Raytracer

Through my work on this project, I gained experience in areas important for optimizing games, such as multi-threading, caches and GGPU (General Purpose Graphics Processing Unit). By implementing these techniques and technologies, I were able to create a highly efficient raytracer that can handle tens of thousands of objects with ease, while also supporting advanced features such as reflections. To achieve the high performance for this raytracer, I employed a BVH, as well as a subdivisible screen-space grid to quickly eliminate areas that do not require raytracing.
